Trophies Won by India

Trophy Year Details
ICC Cricket World Cup 1983, 2011 India won its first World Cup in 1983 under Kapil Dev's captaincy and again in 2011 under MS Dhoni's leadership.
ICC T20 World Cup 2007 India won the inaugural T20 World Cup in 2007 under MS Dhoni's captaincy.
ICC Champions Trophy 2002, 2013 India shared the trophy with Sri Lanka in 2002 and won it outright in 2013 under MS Dhoni's captaincy.
Asia Cup 1984, 1988, 1990-91, 1995, 2010, 2016, 2018,2023 India has won the Asia Cup multiple times, demonstrating dominance in the regional tournament.
Border-Gavaskar Trophy 2001, 2008, 2010, 2013, 2017, 2021,2023 India has won this Test series against Australia multiple times, with notable victories both home and away.
ICC U19 Cricket World Cup 2000, 2008, 2012, 2018, 2022 India has a strong record in youth cricket, winning the U19 World Cup five times.
